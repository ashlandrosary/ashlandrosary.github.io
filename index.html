Details

After Holy Mass, the Holy Rosary of the Blessed Virgin Mary is the most important and consistently powerful prayer in the world. Our Mother in Heaven has repeatedly pleaded that all Catholics pray the Rosary at least once every day. Hence, Ashland Catholic has started a daily Rosary event at 4:00pm located at The Blessed Virgin statue beside St. Charles Borromeo Church in Ashland, Pennsylvania. When the clock strikes four, Peter Schachte will begin the Rosary (Five decade version). If Peter is nowhere to be seen at the time, any person may lead the prayer in his stead. Prayer intentions may be requested to Peter in person or through email using the button above.

Led and organized by Peter Schachte

"I was born in Harrisburg and moved to Ashland at the age of four. I have a strong commitment to this parish community and will stop at nothing to leave this world better than I found it."
